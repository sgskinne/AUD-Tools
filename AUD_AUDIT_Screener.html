Alcohol Use Disorders Identification Test (WHO)

10 questions about your drinking over the past 12 months. Takes ~3 minutes. Completely anonymous — your answers never leave your device. Tap the option that best describes you for each question.

This tool is for educational purposes only and is not a clinical diagnosis. If you have concerns about your drinking, speak with a healthcare provider or contact SAMHSA: 1-800-662-4357 (free, confidential, 24/7).

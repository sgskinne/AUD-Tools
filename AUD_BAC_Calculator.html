Widmark Formula:
BAC = (Drinks × 14 g) ÷ (Weightkg × r × 10) − (0.015 × Hours)

r = body water constant (0.68 male / 0.55 female)
0.015%/hr = average hepatic elimination rate
⚠️ This is an estimate. Actual BAC varies with food, hydration, medications, genetics, and individual metabolism. The only safe BAC before driving is 0.00%.
